[47] 当 $x\to0^+$ 时成立
[48] $(1)$ $x^{x}-x^{\sin x}\sim1\cdot(x-\sin x)\ln x\sim\frac{x^{3}}{6}\ln x.$
[49] $(2)$ $x^{x}-(\sin x)^{x}\sim1\cdot(x-\sin x)\frac{x}{\sin x}\sim\frac{x^{3}}{6}.$
[50] $(3)$ $x^{\sin x}-(\sin x)^{x}\sim1\cdot(\sin x-x)\ln x\sim\,\frac{-x^{3}}{6}\ln x.$
[51] $(4)$ $x^{x}-(\sin x)^{\sin x}\sim1\cdot(x-\sin x)\ln x\sim\frac{x^{3}}{6}\ln x.$
[52] $$~$$
